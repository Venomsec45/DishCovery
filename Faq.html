Getting Started

How do I create an account on DishCovery?

Creating an account is simple! Click the 'Log In' button in the top navigation, then select 'Sign Up'. You can register with your email address or use your Google/Facebook account for quick access. Once registered, you'll have access to save recipes, create meal plans, and join our community.

Is DishCovery free to use?

Yes! DishCovery offers a comprehensive free tier that includes access to thousands of recipes, basic search functionality, and the ability to save your favorite recipes. We also offer a premium subscription with additional features like advanced meal planning, exclusive recipes, and priority customer support.

How do I search for recipes?

Use our smart search feature to find recipes by ingredients, cuisine type, cooking time, or dietary restrictions. You can also browse by categories like breakfast, lunch, dinner, or desserts. Our advanced filters help you find exactly what you're looking for based on your preferences and available ingredients.

Recipes & Cooking

Are all recipes tested before publication?

Absolutely! Every recipe on DishCovery is thoroughly tested by our culinary team and community of expert home cooks. We ensure that all measurements are accurate, cooking times are precise, and the final results are delicious. Each recipe also includes user ratings and reviews for additional validation.

Can I submit my own recipes?

Yes! We love featuring recipes from our community. Once you have an account, you can submit your original recipes through our recipe submission form. Our editorial team reviews all submissions to ensure they meet our quality standards before publication. Accepted recipes are credited to the original creator.

How can I modify recipes for dietary restrictions?

Many of our recipes include substitution suggestions for common dietary needs like gluten-free, dairy-free, or vegan options. You can also use our dietary filter to find recipes that already meet your specific requirements. Our community often shares successful modifications in the comments section.

What if I don't have all the ingredients for a recipe?

Our recipe pages include suggested substitutions for many ingredients. You can also use our 'Recipe by Ingredients' search feature to find recipes based on what you already have at home. The community comments are also a great resource for ingredient alternatives and modifications.

Account & Features

How do I save my favorite recipes?

Once you're logged in, simply click the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your favorites. You can access all your saved recipes from your profile page. You can also organize them into custom collections like 'Quick Weeknight Dinners' or 'Holiday Desserts'.

Can I create meal plans?

Yes! Our meal planning feature allows you to drag and drop recipes into a weekly calendar. You can plan breakfast, lunch, and dinner for each day, and the system will automatically generate a shopping list based on your planned meals. This feature is available to all registered users.

How do I change my account settings?

Go to your profile page by clicking the account tab in the top navigation. From there, you can update your personal information, change your password, modify notification preferences, and manage your dietary restrictions and cooking preferences.

Technical Support

The website is loading slowly, what should I do?

Slow loading can be caused by various factors. Try clearing your browser cache, disabling browser extensions temporarily, or switching to a different browser. If the problem persists, check your internet connection or try accessing the site from a different device. Contact our support team if issues continue.

I'm having trouble uploading photos for my recipe

Make sure your image files are in JPG, PNG, or GIF format and under 5MB in size. Images should be at least 800x600 pixels for best quality. If you're still having trouble, try resizing your image or using a different browser. Our support team can help with persistent upload issues.

Can I access DishCovery on a mobile device?

Yes! DishCovery is fully responsive and works great on smartphones and tablets.

Community & Safety

How do I join cooking contests and challenges?

We regularly host cooking challenges and contests! Check our homepage and community section for current events. To participate, simply follow the challenge guidelines and submit your entry through the designated form. Winners are announced on our social media channels and website.

Can I block or mute other users?

Yes, you can block users who are bothering you or whose content you don't want to see. Go to their profile page and click the 'Block User' option. Blocked users won't be able to comment on your recipes or send you messages, and you won't see their content in your feed.

How do I report inappropriate content or behavior?

We take community safety seriously. You can report inappropriate content using the 'Report' button found on recipes, comments, and user profiles. You can also contact our moderation team directly through our contact form. All reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken.
